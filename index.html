About: 

 4 years ago, 2 whisky drinking Bristol based DJs went to play in Mexico City and fell in love with mezcal. A plot was hatched to bring together the best of these two beloved beverages and the idea for Mezky was born. 

After a few years of tinkering with various combinations of mezcals, barrel types and ageing times, a winning formula was eventually devised. Each edition of Mezky will be highly limited, as mezcal and similar agave distillates, are generally produced in very small batches in village palenques. 

Similarly, every barrel is carefully selected for the specific flavours the wood yields to the spirit, and of course, what spirits have previously been aged in it beforehand.

Mezky #1 

 This edition of Mezky is a compound of two selected Espadin Joven Mezcals, aged in an Islay whisky quarter cask barrel, over several months. 

 100% Agave distillate, Reposado style 

 Taste: Smooth mezcal hit, followed by notes of vanilla, caramel, sea air and peated smokey goodness.
